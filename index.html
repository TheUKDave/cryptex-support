Cryptex

A daily word puzzle game

How to Play

Spin the letter wheels to transform the starting word into the target word. The rules are simple:

  • Change only one letter at a time
  • Every step must form a valid English word
  • Find the path from start to finish

Tips

  • Stuck? Tap the Hint button to reveal the next word in the solution path.
  • Made a wrong turn? The hint will let you know if you're off the solution path.
  • Want to start over? Tap Reset to return to the starting word.

Frequently Asked Questions

When do new puzzles appear?
A new puzzle is available every day at midnight UTC.

Can I play older puzzles?
Yes! Use the arrows next to the puzzle name to browse the archive of past puzzles.

Is there a limit to hints?
No, you can use as many hints as you need to complete the puzzle.
