--- layout: default title: Privacy Policy --- # Privacy Policy for Cryptex *Last updated: January 2026* Cryptex is a word puzzle game that respects your privacy. ## Data Collection Cryptex does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app: - Does not require an account or login - Does not track your location - Does not use analytics or advertising SDKs - Stores game progress only on your device ## Network Requests The app connects to our server solely to fetch daily puzzles. No personal data is transmitted. ## Contact Questions about this privacy policy? Visit our [support page](https://theukdave.github.io/cryptex-support/).
