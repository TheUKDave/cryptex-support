Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Cryptex is a word puzzle game that respects your privacy.

Data Collection

Cryptex does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app:

  • Does not track your location
  • Does not use analytics or advertising SDKs
  • Stores game progress only on your device

Network Requests

The app connects to our server solely to fetch daily puzzles. No personal data is transmitted.
